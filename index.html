new date

Math examples

Foydalanuvchining joriy vaqtini olish

let currentDate = new Date();
console.log(currentDate);.

Aniq bir sanani yaratish uchun yili, oyi, kuni va boshqa parametrlar

// Oy 0-indeksli (0 - yanvar, 11 - dekabr)
let specificDate = new Date(2024, 7, 29); console.log(specificDate); .

Sana stringini new Date() ga uzatish orqali sana obyektini yaratish mumkin.

let dateString = new Date("2024-yil 29-avgust 14:30:00");
console.log(dateString);

Sanani timestamp (1970-yil 1-yanvardan boshlab hisoblangan millisekundlar) yordamida ham yaratish mumkin.

let timestampDate = new Date(1725100200000);
console.log(timestampDate);
